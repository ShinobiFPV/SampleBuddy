# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

SampleBuddy is a small, hobby-scale project, but everyone interacting with it — filing issues, opening pull requests, commenting on discussions — deserves a respectful, harassment-free experience regardless of experience level, background, or how they use the tool.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in comments, issues, and reviews.
- Assume good faith. Most bug reports and "why doesn't this work" questions are genuine, not criticism.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully, including on rejected pull requests.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks directed at any individual.
- Discriminatory jokes or language.
- Publishing others' private information (e.g. from crash logs or sample filenames) without consent.
- Sustained disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Scope

This applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and in any other context where someone is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported by opening an issue or emailing shinobifpv@gmail.com. Reports will be reviewed and addressed as appropriate, which may include a warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from project spaces, at the maintainer's discretion.
